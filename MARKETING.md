## VALUE PROPOSITION

Quantum Studio v0.9.0-beta is a next-generation design environment that eliminates the friction between creative tools, asset libraries, and AI capabilities. Built for professional designers, developers, and content teams who demand power without complexity.

---

## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

1. **Unified AI Workspace**
   Unlike fragmented toolchains requiring exports between Canva, ChatGPT, and Photoshop, Quantum Studio integrates Google Gemini directly into the canvas. Generate images, write copy, and refine designs without context switching.

2. **Native Collaboration**
   Real-time cursor tracking and presence management built on Supabase Realtime — not bolted-on plugins. See your team's creative process as it happens.

3. **Browser-Native Performance**
   No installation. No updates. Professional-grade canvas manipulation via Fabric.js and WebGL 3D rendering via Three.js, delivered through modern web standards.

4. **Intelligent Asset Pipeline**
   Direct integration with Pexels, Pixabay, and Unsplash eliminates manual download-upload cycles. Search, select, and place in one fluid motion.

5. **Offline-First Reliability**
   idb-keyval caching ensures your work persists through connectivity interruptions. Automatic sync when connection restores.

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

- **Platform:** Web application (browser-based)
- **Frontend:** React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vite
- **Canvas Engine:** Fabric.js 7.4.0 (2D), Three.js 0.184.0 (3D)
- **State:** Zustand 5.0.14
- **Animation:** Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Realtime), Firebase
- **AI:** Google Gemini 2.4.0
- **Browser Support:** Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+
- **Hardware:** WebGL 2.0 capable GPU recommended
